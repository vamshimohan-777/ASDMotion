Data Collection Guide

Actions For Analysis

  • Record baseline stillness for 3-5 seconds before activity.
  • Capture repeated hand and face interaction behaviors naturally.
  • Include seated and standing segments when possible.
  • Avoid fast camera movement; keep subject centered in ROI.
  • Record at least 30-60 seconds per sample.

Camera Position

  • Place camera at chest-to-eye level facing the subject.
  • Maintain 1 to 2 meters distance so full upper body is visible.
  • Keep camera stable on tripod or fixed surface.
  • Avoid extreme side angles; prefer frontal or slight 15 degree angle.

Lighting

  • Use even front lighting across face and hands.
  • Avoid strong backlight from windows behind subject.
  • Reduce flicker sources and rapid light changes.
  • Keep scene brightness consistent for the full recording.
